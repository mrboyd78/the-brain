# Webflow Third-Party Platform Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on profitable third-party app opportunities in the Webflow ecosystem, with emphasis on painful design and content workflows, underserved Webflow user niches, weak Marketplace categories, monetizable governance and workflow gaps, and opportunities created by current Designer Extension and Data API capabilities.

They are tailored for the perspective of a **third-party developer** who wants to build a profitable Webflow app business.

These prompts are designed to investigate:
- painful recurring Webflow workflows,
- underserved user roles and account types,
- weak or outdated Marketplace categories,
- monetizable design, CMS, governance, and content-operation gaps,
- current Designer Extension and Data API surfaces,
- native-platform versus third-party risk,
- monetization and retention logic,
- trust, adoption, and switching barriers,
- ranked opportunity selection.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format
