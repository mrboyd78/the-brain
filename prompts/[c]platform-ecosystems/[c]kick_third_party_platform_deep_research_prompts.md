# Kick Third-Party Platform Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on profitable third-party app opportunities in the Kick ecosystem, with emphasis on painful creator and moderator workflows, underserved channel niches and community types, weak or missing tool categories, monetizable engagement and moderation gaps, and opportunities created by Kick’s current public API and webhook surfaces.

They are tailored for the perspective of a **third-party developer** who wants to build a profitable Kick-native or Kick-adjacent app business.

These prompts are designed to investigate:
- painful recurring creator, moderator, and viewer workflows,
- underserved channel types and community segments,
- weak or unsolved tool categories,
- monetizable chat, moderation, reward, analytics, and engagement gaps,
- Kick’s current public API and webhook surfaces,
- native-platform versus third-party risk,
- monetization and retention logic,
- trust, adoption, and switching barriers,
- ranked opportunity selection.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format
