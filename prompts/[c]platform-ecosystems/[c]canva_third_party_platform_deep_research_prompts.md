# Canva Third-Party Platform Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on profitable third-party app opportunities in the Canva ecosystem, with emphasis on painful creative workflows, underserved user niches and business use cases, weak Marketplace categories, monetizable publishing and data-workflow gaps, and opportunities created by Canva’s current Apps SDK, Connect APIs, and Design Editing API capabilities.

They are tailored for the perspective of a **third-party developer** who wants to build a profitable Canva app or integration business.

These prompts are designed to investigate:
- painful recurring creative and content workflows,
- underserved Canva user roles and business types,
- weak or outdated Canva app categories,
- monetizable creation, publishing, data, and governance gaps,
- current Apps SDK and Connect API surfaces,
- native-platform versus third-party risk,
- monetization and retention logic,
- trust, adoption, and switching barriers,
- ranked opportunity selection.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format
