# Twitch Third-Party Platform Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on profitable third-party app opportunities in the Twitch ecosystem, with emphasis on painful streamer and viewer workflows, underserved creator niches and communities, weak Extension categories, monetizable engagement and moderation gaps, and opportunities created by Twitch’s current Extensions model.

They are tailored for the perspective of a **third-party developer** who wants to build a profitable Twitch extension or adjacent Twitch-native app business.

These prompts are designed to investigate:
- painful recurring streamer, moderator, and viewer workflows,
- underserved channel types and creator communities,
- weak or outdated Extension categories,
- monetizable engagement, configuration, moderation, and community-interaction gaps,
- current Extension surfaces and capabilities,
- native-platform versus third-party risk,
- monetization and retention logic,
- trust, adoption, and switching barriers,
- ranked opportunity selection.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format
