# HubSpot Third-Party Platform Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on profitable third-party app opportunities in the HubSpot ecosystem, with emphasis on painful workflows, underserved teams and niches, weak marketplace categories, monetizable trust and governance problems, and opportunities created by newer or underexploited HubSpot capabilities.

They are tailored for the perspective of a **third-party developer** who wants to build a profitable HubSpot app business.

These prompts are designed to investigate:
- painful operational workflows,
- underserved HubSpot roles and account types,
- weak or outdated Marketplace categories,
- monetizable workflow and automation gaps,
- new or underexploited extension surfaces,
- native-platform versus third-party risk,
- monetization and retention logic,
- trust, security, and adoption barriers,
- ranked opportunity selection.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format
