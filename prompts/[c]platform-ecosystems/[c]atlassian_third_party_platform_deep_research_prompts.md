# Atlassian Third-Party Platform Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on profitable third-party app opportunities in the Atlassian ecosystem, with emphasis on painful Jira and Confluence workflows, underserved teams and admins, weak Marketplace categories, monetizable governance and automation gaps, and opportunities created by the current Forge-first platform transition.

They are tailored for the perspective of a **third-party developer** who wants to build a profitable Atlassian app business.

These prompts are designed to investigate:
- painful operational workflows,
- underserved Jira and Confluence user groups,
- weak or outdated Marketplace categories,
- monetizable workflow, admin, and governance gaps,
- Forge-era extension surfaces,
- Connect-to-Forge transition opportunities,
- native-platform versus third-party risk,
- monetization and retention logic,
- trust, security, and adoption barriers,
- ranked opportunity selection.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
