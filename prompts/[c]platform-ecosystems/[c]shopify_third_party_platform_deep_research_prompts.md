# Shopify Third-Party Platform Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on profitable third-party app opportunities in the Shopify ecosystem, with emphasis on merchant pain points, underserved merchant niches, weak or outdated app categories, monetizable workflow gaps, and opportunities created by newer or underexploited Shopify capabilities.

They are tailored for the perspective of a **third-party developer** who wants to build a profitable Shopify app business.

These prompts are designed to investigate:
- painful merchant workflows,
- underserved merchant segments,
- weak App Store categories,
- Shopify-native versus third-party boundaries,
- new or underexploited extension surfaces,
- monetization and retention logic,
- adoption, trust, and switching barriers,
- ranked opportunity selection.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format
