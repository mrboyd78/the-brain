# Consumer Price Elasticity, Discount Sensitivity, and Promotional Behavior Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on consumer price elasticity, discount sensitivity, promotional behavior, willingness-to-buy thresholds, deal dependence, and promotion-driven profitability conducted entirely through public information and LLM-assisted analysis, without interviews or surveys.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format
