These prompts are designed for deep research on consumer identity, self-image, aspiration, status signaling, symbolic purchase behavior, and identity-linked profitability conducted entirely through public information and LLM-assisted analysis, without interviews or surveys.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format
