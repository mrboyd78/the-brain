These prompts are designed for deep research on consumer switching behavior, replacement triggers, inertia-breaking moments, migration friction, substitution logic, and profitable replacement-entry dynamics conducted entirely through public information and LLM-assisted analysis, without interviews or surveys.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format
