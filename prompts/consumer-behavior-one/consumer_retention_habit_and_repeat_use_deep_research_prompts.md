These prompts are designed for deep research on consumer retention, repeat use, habit formation, ongoing value perception, recurrence economics, and durable customer behavior conducted entirely through public information and LLM-assisted analysis, without interviews or surveys.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:

- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format
