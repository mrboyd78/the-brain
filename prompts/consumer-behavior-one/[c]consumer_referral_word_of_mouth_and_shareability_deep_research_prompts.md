These prompts are designed for deep research on consumer referral behavior, word-of-mouth dynamics, shareability, social transmission, recommendation triggers, and referral-driven profitability conducted entirely through public information and LLM-assisted analysis, without interviews or surveys.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
