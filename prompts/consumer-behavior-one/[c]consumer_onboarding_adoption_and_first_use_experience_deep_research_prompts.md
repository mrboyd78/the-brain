# Consumer Onboarding, Adoption, and First-Use Experience Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on consumer onboarding, adoption, first-use experience, early activation, setup burden, confidence-building, and first-value conversion conducted entirely through public information and LLM-assisted analysis, without interviews or surveys.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format
