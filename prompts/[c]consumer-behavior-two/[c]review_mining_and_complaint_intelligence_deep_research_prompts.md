# Review-Mining and Complaint-Intelligence Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on review mining, complaint intelligence, unmet-need detection, and public sentiment pattern analysis conducted entirely through public information and LLM-assisted analysis, without interviews or surveys.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format
