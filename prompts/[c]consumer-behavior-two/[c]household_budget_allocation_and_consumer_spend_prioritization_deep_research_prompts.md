# Household Budget Allocation and Consumer Spend Prioritization Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on household budget allocation, consumer spend prioritization, category tradeoffs, affordability logic, substitution behavior, and budget-pressure resilience conducted entirely through public information and LLM-assisted analysis, without interviews or surveys.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format

