# Implementation Friction and Time-to-Value Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on implementation friction, onboarding burden, setup complexity, adoption drag, time-to-value compression, and activation design conducted entirely through public information and LLM-assisted analysis, without interviews or surveys.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format
