# Consumer Trust, Risk Perception, and Purchase Confidence Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on consumer trust, risk perception, purchase confidence, credibility formation, uncertainty reduction, and trust-driven profitability conducted entirely through public information and LLM-assisted analysis, without interviews or surveys.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format
