These prompts are designed for deep research on consumer use frequency, intensity of use, value density per session, usage depth, recurrence strength, and profitability implications conducted entirely through public information and LLM-assisted analysis, without interviews or surveys.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format
