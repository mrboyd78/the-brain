# Platform Pricing Power, Budget Ownership, and Monetization Approval Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on **who controls budget, how much pricing power a third-party developer can realistically have, and what approval structure governs monetization** across platform ecosystems.

They are specifically designed for the perspective of a **third-party developer** trying to understand:
- who can actually spend money,
- who approves paid plans or app charges,
- how budget ownership differs from product usage,
- what pricing models fit the platform,
- what pricing models create friction,
- where monetization is platform-constrained,
- where pricing power is strong or weak,
- and how approval structure shapes monetization strategy.

The goal is to avoid a common platform-business mistake:

**mistaking usage demand for pricing power, or assuming a platform supports the same monetization logic as a standalone SaaS product.**

This pack covers the current platform set:
- Shopify
- HubSpot
- Atlassian
- Monday.com
- Webflow
- Canva
- Twitch
- Kick
- Google Workspace Marketplace

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format

## What This Pack Is Supposed to Prevent

This pack is designed to prevent the following strategic mistakes:
- pricing to the user instead of the budget owner
- assuming install authority equals spending authority
- using standalone SaaS pricing logic on a platform with constrained monetization
- underestimating how admin or billing friction weakens price realization
- confusing strong usage demand with real willingness to pay
- ignoring renewal ownership when setting packaging or price metrics
- choosing billing metrics that fight the platform’s natural buying behavior
- underestimating the gap between self-serve enthusiasm and durable budget defense

The goal is to force clarity on who controls money, how price is approved, and where third-party developers actually have or lack pricing power.
