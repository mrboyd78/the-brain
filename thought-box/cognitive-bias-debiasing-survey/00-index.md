## Key Cross-Cutting Findings

- **Bias blind spot** is itself one of the strongest barriers to debiasing — people who are most biased are often least aware of it
- **Structural interventions** (checklists, forced procedures) consistently outperform cognitive training in field settings
- **One-shot awareness training** produces null effects in most field replications — the Morewedge (2015) result uses interactive feedback games, not passive lectures
- **Incentive-aligned forecasting** (Good Judgment Project) is the strongest field-validated debiasing mechanism to date
- **Transfer across domains is weak** — gains from debiasing training are largely task-specific unless the training is procedural rather than cognitive
