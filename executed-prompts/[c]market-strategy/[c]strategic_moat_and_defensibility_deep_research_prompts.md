# Strategic Moat and Defensibility Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on strategic moats, defensibility mechanisms, displacement resistance, and long-term competitive durability conducted entirely through public information and LLM-assisted analysis, without interviews or surveys.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format

