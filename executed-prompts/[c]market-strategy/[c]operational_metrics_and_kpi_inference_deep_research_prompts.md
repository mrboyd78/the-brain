# Operational Metrics and KPI Inference Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on operational metrics, KPI design, proxy measurement, public-signal inference, and performance-diagnostic reasoning conducted entirely through public information and LLM-assisted analysis, without interviews, surveys, or private dashboards.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format
