# Failure-Mode and SaaS Postmortem Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on failure modes, strategic fragility, category-specific collapse patterns, postmortem inference, and avoidable SaaS mistakes conducted entirely through public information and LLM-assisted analysis, without interviews or surveys.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format

