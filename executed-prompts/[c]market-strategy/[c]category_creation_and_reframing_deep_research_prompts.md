# Category Creation and Reframing Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on category creation, market reframing, problem-definition shifts, narrative advantage, and strategic perception design conducted entirely through public information and LLM-assisted analysis, without interviews or surveys.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format
