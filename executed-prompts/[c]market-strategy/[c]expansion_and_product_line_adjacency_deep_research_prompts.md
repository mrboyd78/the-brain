# Expansion and Product-Line Adjacency Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on product expansion, adjacency selection, land-and-expand strategy, wedge evolution, and product-line design conducted entirely through public information and LLM-assisted analysis, without interviews or surveys.

Each prompt is copy-and-paste ready and includes:
- master operating instructions
- a rigor add-on
- a consistent output format
