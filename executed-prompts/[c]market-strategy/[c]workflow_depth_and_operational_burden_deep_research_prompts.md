# Workflow Depth and Operational Burden Deep Research Prompts

These prompts are designed for deep research on workflow depth, operational burden, exception handling, and burden-transfer opportunity conducted entirely through public information and LLM-assisted analysis, without interviews or surveys.

- a consistent output format
