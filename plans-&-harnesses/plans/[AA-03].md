# Plan [AA-03] - Solopreneur Ops Deep Research

## Objective
Conduct deep research for each prompt in the `solopreneur-ops` folder, excluding internal/specific business prompts. Save results in `research-artifacts` with specific organization and mark/move completed files.

## Workflow
1.  **Extract Prompts**: Parse each file in `prompts/solopreneur-ops` to identify individual research prompts.
2.  **Filter**: Skip prompts related to specific products, business identity, or services.
3.  **Execute Research**: For each valid prompt, perform deep research using the `generalist` sub-agent.
4.  **Save Deliverables**:
    *   Path: `research-artifacts/<3-word-dash-separated-folder>/[subfolder/]<file-name>.md`
    *   Folder names: lowercase, max 3 words, dash-separated.
    *   Max 10 folders per parent folder.
    *   Max 1 level of nesting.
5.  **Completion Marking**:
    *   Append `[c]` to the start of the file name in `prompts/solopreneur-ops` once all its prompts are done.
    *   Append `[c]` to the folder `solopreneur-ops` once all files are done.
    *   Move the `[c]solopreneur-ops` folder to `executed-prompts`.
